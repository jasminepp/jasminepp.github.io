For the first time to participate in this kind of competition, there are so many things I learned

  1. It is necessary to strengthen the learning of the basic language. It is precisely because the foundation is not strong enough that it is impossible to make good use of the algorithms in the literature.
  2. I understand the true meaning of “big data”, and use big data to upgrade and reform products so that the company can develop better sales plans and strategies. Big data can also be applied to other fields
  3. Don’t rush for success. The three-day competition time is very tight, so we quickly sorted out our ideas, but slowly found some loopholes in the process, and then slowly adjusted it. Only step by step can we succeed.
  4. In short, this competition not only sharpened my mind, but also let me know my shortcomings clearly, and inspired me to learn more related knowledge and fight again next year!
